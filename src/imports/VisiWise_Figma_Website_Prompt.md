## INTERACTION & ANIMATION NOTES

- Hero content: fade + slide up on load (staggered, 150ms offset per element)
- Cards (service, pricing, article): stagger fade-in on scroll entry (80ms offset each)
- CTA buttons: scale 1.02 + slight shadow on hover (150ms ease)
- Service cards: lift 4px + accent border on hover
- Article cards: lift 4px + lightened border on hover
- Filter pills: border + text color transition on click (150ms)
- Nav: background solidifies + blur activates after scrolling 80px
- "Read →" and arrow links: gap between text and arrow widens on hover
